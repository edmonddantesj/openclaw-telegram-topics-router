Inside Ralph Loop, longform becomes a repeated **ingest / extract / package / route** loop so that large inputs do not stay as vague backlog blobs.

- model choice / TPM tradeoff rules

## What moves into Ralph Loop
1. intake triage (what to read fully vs summarize vs defer)
2. chunking large documents into executable reading tasks
3. repeated ingest packaging rules
4. routing outputs into research / knowledge / notion / follow-up tasks
5. backlog reduction for large-content queues

- decide target: `context/research/`, `context/knowledge/`, or topic follow-up

### RL-LF-04 Batch discipline
- prevent giant longform backlog by splitting into small packets

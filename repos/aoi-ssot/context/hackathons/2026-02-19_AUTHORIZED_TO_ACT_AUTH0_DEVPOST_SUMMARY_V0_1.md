## Snapshot
- Theme: AI agents in production — addressing the "trust deficit" via identity + secure tool calling.
- Framing: Most agents are stuck in sandboxes because secure access to a user’s digital life (GitHub/Slack/banking) is a major security/infrastructure burden.
- Auth0 positioning: foundational identity layer enabling Secure Tool Calling, Agent Identity, and the emerging MCP.

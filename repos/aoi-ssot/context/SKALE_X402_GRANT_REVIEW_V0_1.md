3) Reporting: what progress updates are required?
4) Definition of “continued development”: minimal acceptable commitment?
5) Proof of SKALE integration: what evidence is required? (repo link, contract address, tx, docs?)
6) IP/branding: any restrictions? required marketing outputs?
